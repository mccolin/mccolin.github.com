The Things Spambots Blast

Jan 6th, 2011 • Posted in Meta, Rants and Raves

I don’t get much in the way of traffic on my blog’s comments, but always have good ‘ol Akismet kicking up lots of spam for me. Normally, it’s your usual, run-of-the-mill, easily-identifiable sex references, but occasionally it’s kind of mean. Case in point:

I used to love reading your blog, but lately it’s been a little boring. I’ll still read it though =)

That’s harsh, spam robot! I’ve just started up blogging again and here you go knocking down my self-esteem. I suppose my blog was more original and interesting for the months upon months that I left it dormant. Now that I’m wasting your time spewing my thoughts into it, it’s a little hard to take, right? Sorry ’bout that.
